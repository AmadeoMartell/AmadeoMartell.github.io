Price List for Cargo Transportation Services in Kazakhstan

At Neva Trans Company, we offer reliable and efficient cargo transportation services across Kazakhstan. Whether you need to move goods locally or long-distance, our team is committed to delivering your cargo safely and on time. Below is a detailed price list for our transportation services based on the type of cargo, distance, and additional requirements.

Standard Cargo Rates

  • Local Transportation (within city limits): Starting from 100 KZT per km.
  • Intercity Transportation (between cities): Starting from 80 KZT per km for distances up to 500 km. For distances above 500 km, 70 KZT per km.
  • Long-Distance Transportation (cross-country): Starting from 60 KZT per km for distances exceeding 1,000 km.

Specialized Cargo Rates

  • Fragile Goods (e.g., electronics, glassware): Additional charge of 10-20% of the base rate per km.
  • Heavy Machinery & Equipment: Additional charge of 15-25% of the base rate per km, depending on weight and size.
  • Perishable Goods (refrigerated transport): Additional charge of 20-30% of the base rate per km.

Additional Services

  • Insurance for Goods: Starting from 0.5% of the total cargo value.
  • Loading and Unloading Assistance: 1,500-2,000 KZT per hour per worker, depending on the complexity of the cargo.
  • Packaging Services: Starting from 500 KZT per package or 10,000 KZT for bulk packaging.

Note:

Prices may vary based on the specific requirements of your cargo and distance. For a detailed quote, feel free to contact us or request a quote through our online form.

We are committed to offering competitive pricing while ensuring the highest level of care for your cargo.
